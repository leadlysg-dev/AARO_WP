How to book your first appointment

Three steps. That's it.

1

Reach out

WhatsApp, call, or fill in the form below. Tell us briefly: your name, your situation (new diagnosis, second opinion, follow-up), and your preferred clinic location.

2

We get back to you

A patient care coordinator replies within the same working day. They'll confirm the right specialist, the appointment slot, and what to bring.

3

Come in for your consultation

Bring your existing reports, imaging and medication list. Your appointment runs 45–60 minutes — enough time for a full review and your questions.
