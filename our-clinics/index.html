Where treatment happens

All radiation therapy is delivered at our Adam Road CSR.

Consultations and follow-up appointments are offered at all six AARO locations — but radiation therapy itself (SBRT, SRS, VMAT, IMRT, brachytherapy) is delivered exclusively at our flagship Centre for Stereotactic Radiosurgery on Adam Road. CSR is purpose-built for advanced radiation therapy: TrueBeam Linac, dedicated planning rooms, brachytherapy suite, and patient lounges.
