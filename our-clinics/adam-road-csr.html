CSR is the region's first dedicated radiation therapy facility. Every technology, every specialist, under one roof — designed around precision and patient comfort in equal measure.

Public transport

Caldecott MRT (Thomson-East Coast Line / Circle Line) is the closest station, approximately a 10-minute walk. Botanic Gardens MRT (Downtown Line / Circle Line) is also within reach. SBS bus services 52, 105, 132, 151, 153, 154, 156, 157, 162, 162M, 167, 167e, 171, 174, 174e, 502, 502A, 518, 518A and 540 stop along Bukit Timah Road and Adam Road.

Parking

A basement carpark is available within the building, with hourly rates of S$1.20 per 30 minutes. Drop-off is permitted at the main entrance. Valet parking is available on request — please call ahead. Limited street parking is available along Adam Road for short visits.

What sets CSR apart

A facility designed around the patient.

  • Elekta linear accelerator — the latest-generation Linac for SBRT, SRS and VMAT delivery, with 6D robotic couch and surface-guided positioning
  • Brachytherapy suite — purpose-built for image-guided HDR brachytherapy, with on-site shielded recovery
  • Private patient lounges — for rest, refreshments and family between sessions, away from the main waiting area
  • On-site planning & QA — physicists and oncologists work in the same building every day, so plan changes and adaptive replanning happen the same day
  • Multidisciplinary review rooms — for tumour board case discussions, pre-treatment planning meetings, and joint consultations with surgical and medical oncology colleagues
  • Discreet patient flow — separate consultation, treatment and recovery zones, designed so that patients in different stages of their journey rarely cross paths
