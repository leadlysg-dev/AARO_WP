Our Specialists

Each AARO oncologist is a sub-specialist, not a generalist.

Our seven specialists were trained at the National Cancer Centre Singapore — the institution that built Singapore's modern radiation oncology programme. Each focuses on a defined cancer area, so your case is handled by someone whose practice is dedicated to it.

Beyond the doctors

The full clinical team.

Treatment delivery is a team sport. Behind every consultation is a coordinated team of medical physicists, radiation therapists, oncology nurses and care coordinators.

  • Medical Physicists — plan optimisation, dose verification, machine QA
  • Radiation Therapists — daily treatment delivery, image guidance
  • Oncology Nurses — patient education, side-effect management
  • Care Coordinators — scheduling, insurance, your single point of contact
