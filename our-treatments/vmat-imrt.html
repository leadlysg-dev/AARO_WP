What it is

Volumetric Modulated Arc Therapy / Intensity-Modulated Radiation Therapy, in plain language.

VMAT and IMRT deliver radiation in continuously shaped beams that conform tightly to the tumour, minimising dose to surrounding organs.

Cost & insurance

Is VMAT / IMRT covered?

VMAT / IMRT is generally covered under MediShield Life and Medisave for approved indications, and under most Integrated Shield Plans (cashless billing available). Out-of-pocket costs vary based on case complexity and your specific plan.

We provide a written cost estimate at consultation, before treatment begins.
