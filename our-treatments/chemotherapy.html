What it is

Chemotherapy & Systemic Therapy, in plain language.

Chemotherapy uses drugs to destroy cancer cells throughout the body. Modern chemotherapy is increasingly combined with targeted therapy and immunotherapy for better outcomes and tolerability.

Cost & insurance

Is Chemotherapy covered?

Chemotherapy is generally covered under MediShield Life and Medisave for approved indications, and under most Integrated Shield Plans (cashless billing available). Out-of-pocket costs vary based on case complexity and your specific plan.

We provide a written cost estimate at consultation, before treatment begins.
