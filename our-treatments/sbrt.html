Stereotactic Body Radiation Therapy

SBRT

High-dose, high-precision radiation. Treatment in 3–5 sessions, sparing surrounding healthy tissue.

WhatsApp About SBRT
What it is

Stereotactic Body Radiation Therapy, in plain language.

SBRT delivers an ablative dose of radiation to a precisely defined tumour target in just 3–5 sessions, sparing surrounding healthy tissue. By concentrating dose in a small volume with millimetre precision, SBRT achieves cancer-control rates comparable to surgery for many indications — without an incision, hospital stay, or recovery period.

Who is it for?

SBRT is a treatment option for the following cancers and clinical situations:

  • Lung Cancer — early-stage primaries unsuitable for surgery, or oligometastatic lung disease
  • Liver Cancer — hepatocellular carcinoma not surgically resectable, or limited liver metastases
  • Spine Cancer — primary tumours, metastases, and re-irradiation cases requiring high-precision boost
  • Prostate Cancer — low- and intermediate-risk localised disease in 5 sessions
  • Oligometastatic disease — patients with 1–5 metastases where ablating each may improve outcomes

Cost & insurance

Is SBRT covered?

SBRT is generally covered under MediShield Life and Medisave for approved indications, and under most Integrated Shield Plans (cashless billing available). Out-of-pocket costs vary based on case complexity and your specific plan.

We provide a written cost estimate at consultation, before treatment begins.
