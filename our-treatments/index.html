The full radiation toolkit, under one roof.

Choosing the right treatment

Modality matching, not modality marketing.

Different radiation modalities suit different clinical situations. SBRT is often the right choice for small, well-defined targets where dose-escalation matters. VMAT/IMRT is the workhorse for larger volumes and complex anatomy. Brachytherapy is essential for many gynaecological cases. SRS is the standard for brain metastases.

At AARO, your specialist recommends the modality that fits your tumour — not the one we happen to advertise. If we believe a treatment we don't offer is right for you, we'll tell you that too, and help you access it.
