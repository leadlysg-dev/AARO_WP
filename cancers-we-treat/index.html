Cancers We Treat

Twelve cancer types. Specialist focus on each.

AARO treats every major solid-tumour cancer plus haematological cancers requiring radiation. Click any cancer below for our diagnostic approach, treatment options, and the specialists who lead that area.

All cancer types

Find your cancer.

Breast Cancer

The most commonly diagnosed cancer in Singaporean women.

Prostate Cancer

The most commonly diagnosed cancer in Singaporean men.

Lung Cancer

Singapore's leading cause of cancer death — and one where SBRT can be curative.

Brain Cancer

Primary brain tumours and brain metastases — where precision is everything.

Liver Cancer

Hepatocellular carcinoma and metastatic liver disease — including options when surgery is not feasible.

Spine Cancer

Primary and metastatic spinal tumours — including re-irradiation cases.

Colorectal Cancer

Cancer of the colon, rectum, and anus — locally advanced and metastatic.

Gastrointestinal Cancer

Stomach, oesophagus, pancreas, gallbladder and bile-duct cancers.

Genitourinary Cancer

Cancers of the kidney, bladder, ureter, urethra and testis.

Gynaecological Cancer

Cervical, endometrial, ovarian, vulvar and vaginal cancers.

Head & Neck Cancer

Tumours of the oral cavity, throat, larynx, nasopharynx, salivary glands, and thyroid.

Secondary Lymph Node Cancers & Lymphomas

Hodgkin and non-Hodgkin lymphoma, plus secondary nodal involvement from other cancers.
If your cancer isn't listed

Other cancer types

The cancers above are those we treat most frequently — but radiation oncology applies to many other diagnoses, including rarer tumours. If your cancer isn't listed above, please WhatsApp or call us. Our specialists will confirm whether radiation therapy is part of the standard treatment for your case, and which AARO doctor would lead it.
