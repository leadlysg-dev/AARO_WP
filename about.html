Our Story

Singapore's first
independent radiation oncology group.

Founded in 2015 by oncologists from the National Cancer Centre Singapore, AARO brought the academic-centre model — one specialist per cancer type — into the private sector.

Our Mission

Personalised, technology-led cancer care.

We deliver personalised, high-quality cancer care, made possible by technology.

Our Vision

Asia's leading advanced-technology cancer centre.

To be Singapore and Asia's leading advanced-technology cancer centre — offering every patient the full range of treatment options, matched to their condition and preferences.

Who we are

A specialist group, anchored by our own facility.

Founded in 2015, AARO was Singapore's first independent radiation oncology clinic. We've since grown into a full oncology group anchored by our own Centre for Stereotactic Radiosurgery on Adam Road — one team, one place, every modality.

What we do

Our oncologists are trained in both chemotherapy and radiation therapy, so we can recommend the right combination — or sequence — for your specific tumour. Every patient is paired with a sub-specialist whose practice is dedicated to their cancer type, not a generalist managing a wide caseload.

How we work

Multidisciplinary by default.

Every case is reviewed by the relevant sub-specialty team — radiation oncologist, medical physicist, radiation therapist, nurse — before the treatment plan is finalised. Complex cases are taken to a wider tumour board.

This is the academic-centre standard: how cases were handled at NCCS, and how we have continued to handle them since founding AARO.

Company profile

From NCCS pioneers to a regional specialist group.

The milestones that shaped AARO — and the regional radiation oncology programmes our team helped build along the way.

2010

Pioneered Singapore's SBRT programmes

While at the National Cancer Centre Singapore (NCCS), Dr Daniel Tan and team set up SBRT programmes for multiple cancers — treatments now adopted across the region.

2015

AARO is founded

AARO is founded as Singapore's first sub-specialty radiation oncology private practice. Until then, private radiation oncology in Singapore was led by single generalists; AARO brought the academic-centre model — one specialist per cancer type — into the private sector. Today our doctors practise across Parkway, Farrer Park, Mount Alvernia and our own AARO@Adam facility, and consult with UPMC and MD Anderson on regional clinical standards.

2015

Medscan Moscow consultancy

AARO designed, commissioned and operationalised a 15,000 sq ft outpatient oncology centre in Moscow, including TrueBeam Linac. The site passed UPMC accreditation in 2017 and now carries 'in collaboration with UPMC' branding; AARO continues to support its plan reviews and clinical development.

2016

Pinlon Hospital, Yangon

With UPMC, AARO led a two-year programme to upskill Pinlon's radiation team for new IMRT capability — building workflows, protocols and QA processes. Pinlon is now the only hospital in Myanmar delivering routine IMRT.

2016

Iskandar, Malaysia

With Asia American Medical Group (AAMG), AARO co-developed plans for a comprehensive cancer centre commissioned by HRH the Sultan of Johor in memory of his late son. AARO led the service-line, medical-planning and facility-design workstreams. Land was acquired in 2018; construction was scheduled for 2019.

2018

Promed Shanghai

After AARO's own accreditation in Singapore, Concord Medical Services engaged us to bring MD Anderson's protocols into their newly acquired Shanghai outpatient cancer centre, preparing it for international accreditation.
