Financing your treatment

Most of your costs are covered.

AARO's treatments are claimable under MediShield Life, Medisave, and all major Integrated Shield Plans. Cashless billing is available where applicable. Here's what to expect.

Cost

How much will treatment cost?

It depends on the treatment, the duration, and your case complexity. We provide a written estimate at consultation, before treatment begins.

The cashless billing flow

How payment actually works.

1

Pre-authorisation

Once your treatment is agreed, our finance team submits a pre-authorisation request to your insurer with the full clinical justification and cost estimate.

2

Approval & deductibles

Your insurer confirms coverage. We tell you in writing what's covered, what your deductible/co-payment is, and the expected out-of-pocket figure.

3

Treatment proceeds

You go through treatment. The insurer is billed directly for covered amounts. You only pay the deductible / co-payment portion.

4

Paperwork after treatment

We handle claim documentation. Most patients only sign once at admission — no chasing receipts after.

Have a specific cost question?

Our finance team will run an estimate for you. No obligation.
