Our story

An independent radiation oncology practice.

AARO was founded in 2015 by four oncologists from the National Cancer Centre Singapore. We came together around one idea: that patients deserve a sub-specialist for their specific cancer — someone who has spent their career with that disease — paired with the time and continuity that good care requires.

Today we practise from the Centre for Stereotactic Radiosurgery on Adam Road, a facility built specifically for radiation therapy. Our founding team's earlier work at NCCS helped establish stereotactic radiation services in Singapore — experience that continues to inform how we care for patients today.

We are an independent practice of four specialists, each with their own sub-specialty focus. Decisions about your care are made between your oncologist and you — without a hospital group or corporate layer in between.
