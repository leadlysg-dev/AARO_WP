Inside AARO

A clinic that doesn't feel like one.

Our purpose-built facility — the Centre for Stereotactic Radiosurgery on Adam Road — was designed to lower the temperature of every visit. Quiet rooms, a private lounge, and a flow that respects what you came in carrying.

  • Dedicated Elekta linear accelerator and CT-simulation on site
  • Private patient lounge — refreshments, work area, quiet space
  • Same-day consultation slots reserved for new patients
  • The region's first independent radiation therapy facility
